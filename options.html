NaviGestures is a free and open-source extension focused on privacy and minimal resource usage.

There are no ads, no tracking, and no telemetry or data collection of any kind. Ever.

Source code: https://github.com/NaviGestures/NaviGestures

Documentation: https://github.com/NaviGestures/NaviGestures/wiki

Homepage: https://navicorp.net/oss/NaviGestures

Use direction tokens: U D L R UL UR DL DR

Examples: D U, UR, D,R
